QUICK FIX FOR YOUR ERROR:

The problem: You ran uvicorn outside the virtual environment.

Solution: Always activate venv first!

1. cd /Users/yoshikondo/awesome-generative-ai/repoboard
2. source venv/bin/activate
3. cd api
4. uvicorn main:app --reload

OR use the helper script:
./START_API.sh

That's it! The dependencies are already installed in venv.
